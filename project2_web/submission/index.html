Warbow

Having found his place as a squire to a Norman knight, Roland Inness sets off with Sir Roger de Laval and fellow squire on a perilous mission to the Holy Land. King Richard has sworn to take Jerusalem from Saladin and orders Sir Roger to assess the military situation. At battle in the east, Roland sees the ugly face of war and tests his mettle.


The second book in the bestselling series The Saga of Roland Inness!

The Broken Realm

Roland Inness has been knighted for valor at the siege of Acre and sent home to England with a vital message for Queen Eleanor. But his homecoming proves to be as violent and dangerous as the Crusade he left behind him. He finds his home burned and his master’s family gone—made fugitives by his sworn enemy William de Ferrers.


The third book in the bestselling series The Saga of Roland Inness!

The Ransomed Crown

King Richard has made peace with Saladin, but must run a gauntlet of enemies to get home from the Crusade. While England waits for her King, Prince John spares neither lives nor fortune to usurp his brother’s crown. Roland Inness finds himself at the centre of a growing civil war. Who will ultimately sit on the throne of England?

A Prince of Wales

With King Richard’s return from captivity, a weary peace settles on England after years of civil war. But across the border in Wales, the vicious struggle to decide who shall rule Gwynedd still rages. The rebel Prince, Llywelyn, calls in an old debt from the Earl of Chester to send Sir Roland Inness to help him take back his country.


The fifth book in the bestselling series The Saga of Roland Inness!

Declan O'Duinne

With King Richard making war on the French and Prince Llywelyn consolidating power in Wales, peace prevails across England and the Welsh Marches. But bloody strife rages in the north of Ireland, drawing Declan O’Duinne back to his homeland. The Anglo-Normans expand their Irish domains while the Irish kings fight amongst themselves. Can Declan and his fellow knight, Roland Inness, help save free Ireland?

Can Roland and the Invalids survive the war in France with their reputations intact?
